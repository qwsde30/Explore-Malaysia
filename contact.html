Frequently Asked Questions

Click on any question to see the answer

How can I book a tour?

You can fill out the contact form with "Tour Booking" as the subject. We'll respond within 24 hours with available options and pricing!

What travel packages do you offer?

We offer various packages including island getaways, heritage tours, and city escapes. Our packages range from budget to luxury options, all customizable to your preferences.

Do you provide transportation services?

Yes! We offer airport transfers, car rentals, and even chauffeur-driven services throughout Malaysia. Our vehicles range from economy cars to luxury vans.

How do I inquire about partnerships?

Just choose "Partnership / Collaboration" under Category in the contact form and share your proposal. Our business development team will review it and get back to you.

What if I have complaints or feedback?

We value your input! Use the "Feedback / Complaints" category and be as detailed as possible. Our customer service team will investigate and respond with a resolution.

Do you collaborate with influencers?

Yes, we love working with creators! Drop us your media kit under "Media Inquiry" and tell us about your audience and content style. We offer sponsored trips and commission-based partnerships.

Can I advertise on Explore Malaysia?

Absolutely! Select "Advertising / Sponsorship" and tell us your goals—we'll get back with options including banner ads, sponsored content, and newsletter features.

How do I make a payment for a tour or service?

Once your booking is confirmed, we will send you an invoice with payment details. We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and e-wallets. All transactions are secure.
